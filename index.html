PREVIOUS EXPERIENCE
OF MYSELF

Over the past few years, I, Wei, have actively participated in multiple indie game projects, taking on various roles and responsibilities. My journey started with Project Echo, a small-scale sci-fi puzzle game where I worked as a programmer, focusing on implementing core mechanics and refining physics-based interactions.

Later, I joined Shadowdawn, a narrative-driven horror game, as a technical artist, where I contributed to optimizing visual effects and integrating shaders to enhance the eerie atmosphere. At the same time, I collaborated with a team on Pixel Builders, a retro-style engine, where I played the role of a game designer, helping to refine procedural level generation and balance combat mechanics.

Besides these larger projects, I frequently participated in Game Jams like the Global Game Jam and Ludum Dare, where I rapidly developed prototypes within tight deadlines. These experiences pushed me to experiment with different game mechanics and work efficiently within small, dynamic teams.

Through these diverse projects, I have honed my skills in game programming, design, and technical art, making me a versatile developer with a deep passion for indie game creation.
